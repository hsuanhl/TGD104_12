Discover the Joy of Drawing: A Beginner's Guide to Techniques and Materials

  • Speaker :

    Jasper Stone

  • Host :

    Isabella Rose

  • Date & Time :

    01/12 14:00 - 15:30

    &

    01/31 15:30 - 16:00

  • Ticket :

    Free

This event is designed for anyone who is interested in learning how to draw or who wants to improve their drawing skills. Whether you are a complete beginner or have some experience with drawing, this event will provide you with the tools and techniques you need to get started.

You'll have the opportunity to work with a variety of drawing materials, including pencils, charcoal, and ink, and learn how to use them to create different effects. Our experienced instructor will guide you through the process of creating your own drawings, from planning your composition to adding details and finishing touches.

This event is suitable for all skill levels, and no prior experience is necessary. Just bring your curiosity and a willingness to learn, and we'll provide the rest!
