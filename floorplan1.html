Sculpture Garden

The museum's sculpture garden is a peaceful outdoor space featuring works by local artists. Visitors can stroll through the garden and admire the sculptures at their leisure.

The Main Showroom

This large, open space is the main exhibition area of the museum. It features high ceilings and natural light, providing the perfect setting for a wide range of artworks. The Main Showroom is typically used to display the museum's most significant pieces, including large-scale sculptures, paintings and installations.

3

The Historical Room

This room is dedicated to showcasing the museum's historical collection. It is designed to resemble a period-specific setting and it often includes interactive exhibits and multimedia displays to

4

The Intimate Room

This cozy room is designed for small-scale works and more delicate pieces that require a more focused and contemplative viewing experience. The room is dimly lit, with comfortable seating and a soothing ambiance, perfect for visitors to take their time and study the artworks in detail.

5

Gift Shop

The museum's gift shop is the perfect place to find unique souvenirs and art-themed merchandise. From handmade jewelry to reproductions of famous works, there is something for everyone.

6

Cafe and Restaurant

The museum's cafeteria offers a variety of delicious meals and snacks to fuel your visit. From sandwiches and salads to pastries and coffee, there is something for every appetite.
