Parking Info

Welcome to the Museum of Visionary Art! We are excited to have you visit our galleries and exhibits.

Parking Lot 1 :arrow_drop_down

To make your visit as convenient as possible, we offer several parking options for our visitors. Our main parking option is the museum's parking garage located directly next to the museum. This garage offers hourly and daily parking rates. The hourly rate is $3.00 per hour with a daily maximum of $25.00. If you plan to stay longer, you can purchase a weekly pass for $75.00 or a monthly pass for $200.00.

Parking Lot 2 :arrow_drop_down

For those who prefer street parking, we have several metered spots located within a short walking distance from the museum. The hourly rate for street parking is $2.00 per hour with a daily maximum of $15.00. Please note that street parking is limited and subject to availability.

Parking Lot 3 :arrow_drop_down

Please note that parking is not allowed in the museum's loading dock or reserved spots. Any violators will be subject to fines and penalties.If you have any questions or concerns regarding parking, please do not hesitate to ask one of our staff members or contact us.
