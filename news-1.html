Museum Store New Year Sale

The Museum of Visionary Art is excited to announce a special Museum Store Sale, offering a wide selection of unique and inspired gifts, perfect for art lovers and collectors alike. Visitors will have the chance to take home a piece of visionary art from the museum's collection, including hand-made jewelry, limited-edition prints, and handmade sculptures.

The sale will take place from January 1st to January 30th, and will feature exclusive discounts on select items. Visitors can also browse a special selection of books and merchandise inspired by the museum's current exhibitions and permanent collection. Whether you're looking for a gift for a loved one or a special treat for yourself, this is a not-to-be-missed opportunity to take home a piece of visionary art.

So come and experience the magic of visionary art while supporting the Museum of Visionary Art. We look forward to seeing you soon!
