COVID-19 Safety Measures in Museums: Guidelines for Visitors and Staff

To ensure the safety of visitors and staff, all museums are required to implement strict COVID-19 safety measures. This includes mandatory face mask usage for all visitors and staff, as well as regular temperature checks at the entrance.

To limit the number of people inside the museum at any given time, museums will implement a reservation system and may limit the number of visitors allowed in certain areas or exhibitions. Visitors are encouraged to plan their visit in advance and purchase tickets online.

Hand sanitizing stations will be placed throughout the museum and visitors are encouraged to use them regularly. Visitors are also asked to maintain a safe distance of at least 6 feet from other visitors and staff at all times.

Museums will also increase cleaning and sanitation efforts, particularly in high-touch areas such as restrooms, elevators, and handrails. Staff will also be trained on proper cleaning and disinfection protocols. Visitors are also encouraged to stay home if they are feeling unwell or have been in close contact with someone who has tested positive for COVID-19.
