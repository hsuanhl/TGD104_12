Artist Talk Series Announced

The Museum of Visionary Art is excited to announce its new Artist Talk Series, a program of special events featuring some of the most exciting and innovative artists in the visionary art community. Visitors will have the opportunity to engage with artists and learn about their creative processes, inspirations, and the stories behind their visionary works of art.

The Artist Talk Series will run from June 1st to August 31st, and will feature a different artist each week. The talks will be held in the museum's auditorium, and will be open to the public, with a limited number of tickets available each day. Visitors will have the chance to hear from artists working in a variety of mediums, from painting and sculpture to digital and performance art.

So join us for this exciting new series and experience the vibrant and diverse world of visionary art. We look forward to seeing you soon!
